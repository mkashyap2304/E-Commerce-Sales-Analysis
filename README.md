# Retail Sales Performance & Customer Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover trends, customer behavior, and business opportunities to improve revenue and performance.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To identify key sales drivers, customer segments, and seasonal patterns to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Dataset
- Source: Kaggle
- Records: 9,800+ transactions
- Features: Sales, Customer, Region, Category, Order Date, etc.

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Total sales reached approximately **2.26M**
- Peak sales observed in **November**, indicating strong seasonal demand
- **Consumer segment** contributes the highest revenue
- Top customers contribute a significant portion of total sales
- Certain categories and sub-categories dominate performance

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts during peak sales periods
- Strengthen retention strategies for high-value customers
- Expand operations in high-performing regions
- Optimize product portfolio by focusing on top-performing products

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data analysis can be used to generate actionable insights and support business decision-making in a retail environment.
